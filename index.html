At Aarong, we are dedicated to showcasing the rich heritage of Bangladesh through exquisite craftsmanship and timeless designs. As a brand deeply rooted in the cultural tapestry of this vibrant nation, we invite you to embark on a journey where tradition meets contemporary elegance.Established in 1978 as a humble initiative to empower rural artisans, Aarong has evolved into Bangladesh's leading lifestyle brand. Our journey is a testament to our commitment to preserving and promoting traditional crafts while fostering sustainable livelihoods for communities across the country.At the heart of Aarong lies a celebration of craftsmanship. Each product in our collection is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans using age-old techniques passed down through generations. From intricate hand-embroidery to vibrant handloom weaves, every piece tells a story of artistry and dedication.




Ethical Sourcing

We believe in the power of ethical sourcing to create positive change. By partnering directly with artisans and cooperatives, we ensure fair wages and dignified working conditions. Our commitment to sustainability extends to our materials, with a focus on natural fibers and eco-friendly practices. Beyond commerce, Aarong is a catalyst for social change. Through initiatives in education, healthcare, and skill development, we empower communities to thrive and prosper. Every purchase you make directly contributes to these efforts, making a meaningful difference in the lives of countless individuals.
